More About Us

Our Objectives

  • Objectives of Save A Teacher Foundation. To reduce the gap amongst teachers
  • To increase the teachers' abilities to handle emergencies.
  • To improve teachers' abilities to raise income
  • To boost the teachers' dignity
  • To enhance solidarity among teachers in both peaceful and hard times.
  • To boost our network with internal and external partners in order to achieve our objectives.
  • Do all other lawful activities for the attainment of the above objectives.

Our Core Values

  • Unity
  • Empathy
  • Sympathy
  • Respect
  • Confidentiality
  • Transparency
  • Co-operation
  • Discipline
  • Accountability

Our Principles

Below are the principles we base on while conducting our duties at Save A Teacher Foundation

Vision

To transform teachers' mindsets towards saving each other.

Mission

To unite teachers to save one another for effective service.

Aim

To save a teacher in emergencies beyond the source of income of a teacher at a particular moment.

Motto

The Motto of our organisation is "Save at teacher is a call."
